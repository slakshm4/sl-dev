I'm Shradha Lakshman, a dental hygiene student at UBC passionate about health education and innovation.

I'm the founder and CEO of Eureka Enterprises, where I'm developing Eureka: QuestScapes - a gamified web-based application for HIV/AIDS education among Canadian youth. Health Innovation

EUREKA!

QuestScapes Initiative

GAMIFIED HIV/AIDS EDUCATION FOR YOUTH

As founder and CEO of Eureka Enterprises, I'm developing Eureka:QuestScapes - an innovative gamified web-based application designed to educate Canadian youth about HIV/AIDS. This initiative secured an $800 bursary from North American non-profit LetsStopAIDS for its compelling approach to health education. By integrating interactive storytelling and trivia-based combat mechanics into a nostalgic 2D platformer, Eureka:QuestScapes aims to reduce stigma, increase health literacy, and promote preventive behaviors among youth, ultimately contributing to better public health outcomes.
